A multimedia studio built on passion

GTC is a Kigali-based multimedia company dedicated to capturing life's most important moments. From intimate wedding ceremonies to corporate milestones and creative studio portraits, we bring every story to life with precision and artistry.

Beyond photography, we specialize in videography, custom photo printing, laser photo frames, and graphic design — everything you need to preserve and present your memories beautifully.
